About me

Welcome, I'm Rony Hanna, I like creating video games. I have significant experience in game development with particular interest in graphics programming. I'm currently a full time Unity Developer at Kai's Clan in Auckland working on a multiplayer educational game. Previously I worked as a full time Junior Engine Developer at Digital Confectioners in Christchurch. I earned a bachelor's degree in Software Engineering from Media Design School. You can view a few of my projects here. Feel free to get in touch, I'd be happy to chat about job opportunities and projects I've worked on.

Projects

Titan Voyager is a first-person shooter game written in C++ and modern OpenGL (watch gameplay footage here). This project captured some interest on reddit and was discussed here. The game takes place on a mysterious Saturnian moon where the player has to retrieve some important data while also fighting enemies.

Slimey Jump is a 2D platformer game similar to Doodle Jump written in C++ and modern OpenGL. The player has to guide a slime up a series of platforms without falling or colliding with an enemy. The enemies are accompanied with a growling sound and can be shot by the player.

District Underground is a Third-person action game made in Unity. This project was a group project consisting of 3 artists and 3 programmers. I worked as a gameplay programmer and created a number of gameplay mechanics for the game.

An Angry Birds clone written in C++ and powered by the Box2D physics engine. This project was developed as part of a 2nd-year university physics assignment. The demo features two simple levels, three different Box2D joints, and three different birds with different mechanics.

A small pathfinding demo I made in my free time in Unity which features different types of search algorithms. The demo features a Breadth-first traversal, Depth-first traversal, Dijkstra's algorithm, and A*.

Lava Plaza is a simple game made in Unity where the player has to dodge boulders for as long as possible and avoid falling into the lava. This was a group project where I worked as a gameplay programmer and implemented several gameplay mechanics for the game.

Game Jams

On The Beak is a fun multiplayer game where players play as penguins attempting to knock each other off the slippery iceberg. The game was made during KiwiJam 2020 where the theme was "On The Brink". I worked as a programmer alongside 2 others.

Helping Hands is a puzzle game developed during Global Game Jam 2020 where the theme was "repair". The player plays as a misunderstood raccoon who wishes to prove he's a good citizen by roaming the local town and repairing what's broken. I worked as a programmer in a group of 6 people.
